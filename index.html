About

Hello! My name is Feifei Kaglic, and I am a student at UNC-Chapel Hill studying Information Science with a special interest in human-computer interaction.

My primary goal as a future User Experience Designer is to create a more equitable environment by developing products that would better serve minority populations, especially women of color. I endeavor to conduct inclusive user research, gathering both qualitative and quantitative data regarding the pain points of minority populations, and ushering in products that would alleviate those struggles. I view my future role as one that strives to promote human well-being and celebrate diversity. Lately, I have also been interested in narrative design for video games.

Outside of school, I revel in a variety of creative pursuits ranging from writing to multimedia production to digital design.

My earliest memories revolve around spinning tales that starred my toys as characters. I began writing stories at the age of eleven and have not stopped since. Whether it is an intergenerational family saga, a cyberpunk adventure, or a xianxia romance; all of my works relay the message that the blossom of hope will survive even in the bleakest of nights, and that everyone has the agency to shape their own narrative. I also write poetry that explore the emotional hues of my psyche. In Spring 2022, I penned an entire memoir of 43,000 words in 40 days chronicling my life story thus far. I worked on a podcast project with two other classmates for a summer class where I authored the episode script and produced the audio file. Additionally, I created a digital non-linear interactive story using Twine in 9 hours for a 2022 maymester class.

In the realm of multimedia production, I love creating concept albums that challenge the status quo. Ever since I was a kid, I had learned to play music on the piano by ear. Naturally, I composed my first songs on the keyboard in November 2021. That month, I singlehanded made an EP consisting of four songs that explore the themes of rebellion and restlessness. I composed both the melodies and the lyrics for all tracks, recorded myself rapping the songs, and taught myself basic audio production of mixing and mastering to lend a professional quality to the EP. I also enjoy creating videos whether it be lyric videos or video edits for my stories.

In the territory of digital design, I use HTML and CSS to create aesthetically driven webpages. As for graphic design; I primarily focus on story mood boards, book covers, and album covers. I also recently started to play with interior book design and typography.

Thank You for stopping by my creative portfolio! I hope you are having a good day.

FROM YANG TO YIN

Poem Published in the 2022 Renaissance Magazine

❝ Ziyang bit into an egg tart. Salivating aromas of minced pork dumplings, sticky rice chicken, and steaming soup teased Rabbit’s nostrils. She stopped herself from picking up the chopsticks lest her appetite spelled a venomous downfall. The waitress Rabbit bribed should be arriving soon.

Ziyang cleared his throat. “Shang District is a neutral territory. Huan Ying Cult doesn’t own Sui Han Park. You shouldn’t spill blood on grounds that don’t belong to either of us.”

Rabbit fisted the lacquered wooden table, rattling the porcelain plates. “Is that so? Then how come your men slaughtered four of my sisters on ‘neutral territory?’ Huan Ying can be likened to bees; we do not sting unprovoked.” ❞
